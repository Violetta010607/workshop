The role AI might play

Exploring Direction

This application uses the front camera of your smartphone to capture the scene in front of you and detects a tag worn by a person. When the tag is detected the application plays back a sound. The audible feedback has a soft sound when in the middle of the screen and  changes in rate (it accelerates) when it is close to the left or right camera's edges.
