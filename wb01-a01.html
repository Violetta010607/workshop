Activity 1

The sight guiding technique

"Sighted guide technique allows both people on the sighted guide team to feel comfortable, safe, and actively walking together."

I have met several pairs during my study, one of them told me why they enjoy walking together.

Alan (a sighted guide) says: "there is much more going on and travelling with Luke is a great occasion to build up our friendship."

Luke (a person being guided) says: "I enjoy walking with Alan because I can walk at regular pace."

Reflect on your connection/partnership

Activity 2

The Ramble Tag

The Ramble Tag is a lightweight harness, designed to be worn on the upper arm of a guide.  It was created to increase pleasure, independence to the blind person, and the guide more confidence while walking together.

I have provided you an arm band I have made, which is similar to the ramble tag.  Take some time to sense it, then wear it on the guide's arm. Have a short walk together.

DIY - Ramble tag 2.0

Using materials you have received and further materials you may find home, work together to enhance the ramble tag.
