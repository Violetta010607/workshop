The role AI might play

Activity 1

Experience 2 small applications

Materials set up:

  • One person has to hold the smartphone you are using for this workbook in front of themselves.
  • The other person has to wear the tangible tag tied to a strap on their arm. You can find the tangible tag in the envelope marked by a square. A tag is a binary drawing composed of geometric shapes. You can sense it using the large tactile version provided in the materials.

The following two pages contain two slightly different applications to detect the tag one person is wearing using the smartphone's camera the other person is holding. When the tag is detected an audible feedback is played back. When you start, change your position in relation to your companion (e.g., move from left to right, or come closer) and experience how the audible feedback changes in relation to one another position and orientation. A brief task description is provided at the top of each page. The Strat application button will lead you to the first application.
