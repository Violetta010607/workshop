The Ramble Tag

The Ramble Tag is a lightweight harness, designed to be worn on the upper arm of a guide.  It was created to increase pleasure, independence to the blind person, and the guide more confidence while walking together.

I have provided you an arm band I have made, which is similar to the ramble tag.  Take some time to sense it, then wear it on the guide's arm. Have a short walk together.

DIY - Ramble tag 2.0

Using materials you have received and further materials you may find home, work together to enhance the ramble tag.
