The role AI might play

Activity 1

Reflect on these applications in relation to your guiding experience

During my studies I have met some pair who told me that while walking together may also happen they physically disengage for some reason (e.g., an unexpected event happening in the surrounding) and both companions may feel disoriented. Has that ever happened to you? How far do you move? How do you move in relation to your companion?

A new wearable AI sensor can detect these moments when you are not in physical contact during a walk. Which kind of information would you be interested in receiving?  What would a sound indicate?  How would it support your communication?
