Reflect on these experiments in relation to your guiding experience

During my earlier research, I met some pairs who told me that while walking together they sometime physically disengage for some reason (e.g., an unexpected event happening in the surroundings). Here the sight impaired person can feel disoriented.

Has that ever happened to you? Do you move or stand still? How do you move in relation to your companion?
