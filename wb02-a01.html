The role AI might play

Activity 4

Better together than apart

A famous philosopher said: “The whole is greater than the sum of its parts”.

Reflect on your experience as a guide and a person being guided. Think about contexts, situations, or actions during sighted guiding where you do something for your companion. It can be something evident (e.g., alerting of a curb), or something else more subtle (like the pause in the conversation in the previous activity). Which action does the other companion take as a response in these moments? In turn express a meaningful situation each and how the other person responses to that.

Add your AI technology

Reflect on these 2 different meaningful moments you have identified. What may AI add in these moments? What does AI suggest you as a possible new action to be taken? Think about these questions in the form: "I would add an AI technology to [what the technology should do]. This will  help [person] to [action to be taken]"

In the Send your enhanced moments form, describe the 2 situations (adding details about who initiates the communication, and your’s companion response) and complete the sentence.
